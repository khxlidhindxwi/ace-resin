★ FAQ

Common questions.

How long does epoxy last?

A properly prepped and poured floor lasts decades. We back every project with a lifetime finish guarantee — if it ever chips, yellows or peels, we repour it.

Is it slippery?

Our standard finish is similar to polished granite. For garages, gyms and commercial kitchens we add an invisible non-slip aggregate to the top coat.

What does it cost?

Pricing depends on square footage, color complexity and floor prep. Tell us about your space on the quote form and we'll send a written estimate.

How long until I can use it?

Walk-on cure is the next day. Full chemical and heat cure inside a week. We hand you a printed care card at the reveal.

Do you travel?

We're based in the Midwest and travel anywhere in the US for the right project. Send us the location and we'll quote it with travel at cost.

Is it safe / non-toxic?

Yes. Once cured, our resin is inert and food-safe. The faint smell during install dissipates within 48 hours of the final coat.
