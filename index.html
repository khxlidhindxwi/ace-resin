★ FAQ

Common questions.

How long does epoxy last?

Properly poured epoxy from a master-craft team lasts 30+ years. Our lifetime guarantee covers chipping, yellowing and peeling for as long as you own the property — and it's transferable to new owners.

Is it slippery?

Our standard finish is rated DCOF 0.42 — the same as polished granite. For garages, gyms and commercial kitchens we add an invisible aggregate that rates DCOF 0.85, exceeding ADA spec.

What does it cost?

Residential floors start around $12/sqft. Counters start at $95/linear foot. Wall-art commissions start at $4,500. Every quote includes the sample kit, prep, pour, top coat and a 4K install film.

How long until I can use it?

Walk-on cure in 14 hours. Full chemical & heat cure at 7 days. We supply a printed care card with your reveal.

Do you travel?

We have install crews in 31 metros. For destination projects (Aspen, Hamptons, Cabo, etc.) we fly the master pour team with two assistants — travel quoted at cost.

Is it safe / non-toxic?

Yes. Once cured, our resin is 0% VOC and food-safe (NSF-51 / FDA 21 CFR 175.300). Smell dissipates in < 48 hrs.
